Instructions for Disabling MAC Address Randomization for Android Devices

{Any Android Version 9 or lower users can directly skip this}

To check your Android Version:

Open the Settings on your phone.

Scroll down until you locate the About tab

(Can be about phone/about device)

Locate your Android Version


Step 1: Navigate to the Wi-Fi settings of your Android Device.

This may appear differently per device.

Step 2: Click on the Gear next to the Wifi name.

Step 3: Open up Advanced settings in the network.

Step 4: Locate the Privacy setting and make sure you are using your Device MAC.
