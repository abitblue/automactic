Instructions for Disabling MAC Address Randomization for Android Devices

{Any Android Version 9 or lower users can directly skip this}

To check your Android Version:

Open the Settings on your phone.

Scroll down until you locate a about tab (Can be about phone/about device)

Locate your Android Version


- Step 1: Navigate to the settings of your Windows 10 device.

- Step 2: Click on Network & Internet

- Step 3: Select the Wifi Tab

- Step 4: Locate "Use Random Hardware Address" and switch it off
