Instructions for Disabling MAC Address Randomization for iOS/iPadOS Devices

Make to sure apply these settings to both ncpsp and JoinForWifi

Step 1: Navigate to the settings app of your iOS device.

Step 2: Click on Wi-Fi

Step 3: Tap the Information button next to JoinForWifi

Step 4: Locate "Private Address" and switch it off

Step 5: Repeat for ncpsp
